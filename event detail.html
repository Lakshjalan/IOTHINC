About this Event

The year 2024 marks a pivotal moment in the history of artificial intelligence. As Large Language Models (LLMs) transition from experimental curiosities to core infrastructure, engineers must navigate a landscape defined by rapid scaling and architectural shifts.

This session dives deep into the practical implications of generative AI for software development. We will discuss the integration of agentic workflows, the evolution of RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architectures, and the ethical considerations that come with deploying autonomous technical assistants at scale.
